# Measuring Success

**Use classic DORA metrics:**

- **Deployment Frequency** – Weekly → Multiple per day
- **Lead Time** – Days → Hours
- **Change Failure Rate** – 15-20% → 5-10%
- **Recovery Time** – Hours → Minutes (Git revert)

<div class="mt-4" />

**Track:**
- Drift detection rate
- Sync success rate
- Audit compliance

<div class="mt-4 text-xs opacity-75">

*The data tells the story. Track before and after adoption.*
